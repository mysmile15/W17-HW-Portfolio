  PROBLEM STATEMENT

MUM (Music Umbrella for Me) was created to address many problems for both the general consumer and music professional. More specifically, music professionals (music artists and producers) do not have the ability to network and expand their audience through a single application.

By creating this “umbrella” app with better management tools and very specific metadata, music professionals will be able to sync (identify, extract, convert, and reuse relevant data), generate reports, and do a number of tasks faster, efficiently, and more competitively through one destination.

  USER INSIGHT

Our research discovered multiple issues with current mobile music apps, specifically for music professionals from content management to searching to syncing to having offline access. The most common element we found in current music apps was the inability to locate specific songs/tracks and other relevant data on the multiple music apps with the multiple subscriptions music professionals use daily.
